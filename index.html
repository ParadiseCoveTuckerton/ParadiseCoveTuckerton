🌊 Welcome to Paradise Cove

Nestled along the waterfront in beautiful Tuckerton, New Jersey — right behind The Grapevine Restaurant — Paradise Cove is a tight-knit community where neighbors become friends and every day feels like a getaway. Whether you're enjoying a sunset from the water's edge or taking a stroll through the neighborhood, there's a quiet charm here that's hard to find anywhere else.

Just 1 mile from the historic Tuckerton Seaport & Baymen's Museum — a 40-acre coastal cultural center on Tuckerton Creek — our community enjoys easy access to one of the Jersey Shore's hidden gems. The Seaport offers scenic creek tours, a water ferry to Beach Haven, hands-on exhibits celebrating New Jersey's maritime heritage, live aquatic displays, classes, and seasonal events for the whole family.

This website is your hub for everything Paradise Cove community. Stay up to date on neighborhood news, local events, and ways to get involved. Whether you're a longtime resident or a neighboring community member looking to connect — you're welcome here.
